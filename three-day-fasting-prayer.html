Day 1

Uploaded: June 14, 2026

🔥 TODAY IS THE DAY! 🔥

Published now. Return to God through repentance, gratitude, and a renewed yes.

Study Notes
See more

Today begins our 3-Day Fast for the Restore Series. We are taking intentional time to return to God, declare restoration, and believe Him for healing, wholeness, and renewal.

  • Repentance
  • Renouncing wrong agreements, covenants, and idols
  • Keeping a heart posture of gratitude
  • Recalling what was lost

Joel 2:12 — “Return to Me with all your heart…”

Fasting directions: 6AM to 6PM, liquids only. After 6PM: soup, smoothies, salads, tuna, light meat.

During the fast: Watch your ear gate, eye gate, and mouth gate. Remove anything you idolize or go to before speaking to God. Modify based on you and what God speaks to you.

This is not only about what we give up. It is about making room to hear God clearly. Return first. Restoration begins there.
